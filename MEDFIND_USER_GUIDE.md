## üì± Patient App (For the Public)

The Patient App (`/app.html`) is designed for speed and emergency use.

### 1. Emergency Mode (SOS)
*   **SOS Button:** Tap the large red **"SOS - FIND HELP NOW"** button on the home screen.
*   **What it does:** Instantly locates the nearest hospital, shows the distance, and provides a "Call Now" button.

### 2. Searching & Filtering
*   **Service Filters:** Tap icons like **Maternity**, **Surgery**, or **Pediatrics** to show only relevant hospitals.
*   **Smart Search:** Type in the search bar to find hospitals by name, district, or service (e.g., "Bo Government Hospital").
*   **Advanced Filters:** Tap "Advanced Filters" to refine by criteria like **Oxygen Available** or **24/7 Power**.

### 3. List & Map Views
*   **List View:** Clean list of hospitals sorted by distance from you.
*   **Map View:** Interactive map showing hospital pins. Green pins indicate high availability; red pins indicate full capacity.

### 4. Language Support
*   **Toggle Language:** Use the **EN / KR** button in the top right of the header to switch the entire app between English and Krio.

## üîê Admin Portal (For Hospital Staff)

The Admin Portal (`/admin.html`) is for updating hospital status.

### 1. Accessing the Portal
*   Click **"Admin Portal"** in the navigation bar.
*   **Login:** Select your hospital's province, district, and facility name. Enter your secure PIN.

### 2. Live Dashboard
*   View your hospital's current status at a glance: **Beds**, **Oxygen**, **Staff**, **Ambulance**.
*   **Status Indicators:** Green checkmarks mean "Available", Red warning signs mean "Unavailable".

### 3. Updating Status
*   **Quick Updates:** Use the one-tap buttons for common updates (e.g., "Full Capacity", "Restocked").
*   **Full Form:** Scroll down to the detailed form to update specific numbers of beds, staffing levels, or add notes.
*   **Save Changes:** Tap "Update Status". All changes are saved to the central database immediately.

### 4. Offline Mode
*   The Admin Portal works **100% offline**. You can make updates without internet.
*   **Sync:** When you reconnect to the internet, your updates are automatically sent to the server.

## üõü Troubleshooting

*   **Location Not Found:** Ensure GPS / Location Services are enabled on your phone.
*   **"Offline" Banner:** The green banner indicates you are offline. The app is fully functional in this mode.
*   **Map Not Loading:** The detailed map requires an internet connection to load tiles initially, but hospital locations are cached.
