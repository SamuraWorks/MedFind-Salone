# MedFind Salone - User Guide

Welcome to MedFind Salone, your unified platform for accessing emergency healthcare services and managing hospital resources in Sierra Leone.

## üè† Home (Landing Page)
The Home page is your starting point.
- **Live Map:** View a real-time map preview of all hospitals.
  - **Search:** Use the search bar in the map sidebar to find specific hospitals instantly.
- **Navigation:**
  - **Find Care:** Click this to launch the **Patient App** for finding services.
  - **Hospital Admin:** Click this to access the **Admin Portal** for hospital staff.
- **Language:** Toggle between **EN** (English) and **KR** (Krio) using the buttons in the top navigation bar.

## üöë Patient App
Designed for the public to find care quickly, even offline.
- **Search:** Enter a hospital name, district, or service (e.g., "Maternity") in the top search bar.
- **Quick Services:** Tap icons like "Emergency," "Maternity," or "Surgery" to filter hospitals instantly.
- **Advanced Filters:** Click "Advanced Filters" to refine by:
  - Beds/Oxygen Availability
  - Surgeons on Duty
  - Ambulance Availability
  - District Selection
- **Emergency Mode (SOS):**
  - Click the large red **SOS** button for immediate help.
  - The app will locate the nearest hospitals.
  - Use the **Call Now** button to dial the hospital directly.
  - Use **Get Directions** to open Google Maps navigation.

## üîê Admin Portal
For authorized hospital staff to update availability.
- **Login:** Select your hospital from the list to log in.
- **Dashboard:** View current stats (Beds, Oxygen, Staff).
- **Quick Updates:** Use one-tap buttons (e.g., "Full Beds," "Oxygen Restored") for rapid status changes.
- **Full Update Form:** Enter detailed numbers for beds, staff, and notes.
- **History:** View a log of all recent updates made to your facility.
- **Offline Sync:** All updates save locally and sync when you reconnect to the internet.

## üåç Language Support
MedFind Salone is fully bilingual. 
- Use the **EN/KR** toggle in the top right corner of any page to switch the entire application between English and Krio.
